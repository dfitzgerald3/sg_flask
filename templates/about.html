About Sentiment Gauge


What is Sentiment Analysis?
Sentiment Analysis is the process of quantifying the "sentiment" or the emotion about a topic by analyzing text from an individual or collection of individuals regarding one or more topics.
How is it Sentiment Analysis Used?
  • We provide quantitative values describing the sentiment of over 400 securities. Theses values can be used to inform a variety of investment decisions. Near-term variations in stock price are heavily influenced by the markets opinion about the security of interest, the sector/industry it resides in, or the products it produces. Leveraging quantitative data that describes market sentiment can potentially improve your ability to make better investment descisions.
  • Note: Using sentiment data is no guarantee of improved return on investment
